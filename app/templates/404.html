Since you are already here, please take some time to read this! Project Piscator is a final-year capstone project by 6 students from University of Wollongong.


This product is developed using Flask with all the various plugins with a PostgreSQL as our database. For most of us, it is our first time building a project from ground up, everyone was relatively new to the Flask framework, PostgreSQL and even Scikit-learn. Our end product will be deployed on Heroku as a paid Dyno so the product will be free but it will only persist for a short time due to hosting costs.


Our source code is available here if you want to take a look.


The project ran for about half a year, with the team gradually picking up new knowledges on the framework and other development technologies. It definitely was not an easy journey to make it thus far from nothing (at least for us!), but hopefully it was a good learning experience for everyone looking to jump into software development upon graduation.


As we are nearing the end of the project, I'd just like to thank everyone in the team for trying their best to pick up technologies they were not familiar with. Not forgetting external contributors that have sent us phishing emails (intentional or not) to train our machine learning model. It has been a long journey and I hope everyone had something to take away from this project. Happy graduation everyone!
